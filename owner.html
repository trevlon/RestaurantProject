James Fai

Kimmy Fai, James mother oponed thai valley in the 1980's, she put all the money she had in opening this restaurant. Years later since running it for several decades she sold Thai Valley to her son. Who has now modernized it but also keeps their tradition alive. He hired well known chefs from around the world who specialize in thai cooking, and now has the most popular thai restaurant in New Yorke.
