Angie Anuman

Angie Anuman grew up in a small village with a big dream of becoming a chef. When she turned 18 she worked three jobs so she could pay for her studies in a cooking school. After graduating in 2012, she found minor job as a cook. Until one day a food youtuber went to the restaurant while he was on his journey trying different foods and he loved the food there. He showed her in the video, and that's how we found her and added her to the team.

Hymie Athit

Hymie Athit grew up with a long line of chefs in his family, and he decided to follow the same path. He was well educated and got through using some of his dad links. Aside from the help he was still an amazing chef as both his father and grandfather teach him hear and there through his childhood. He went on to be expert in thai culture and thai food cooking and has one the award of 'BEST COOK OF THE YEAR'. He is now the manager of one of our branches.

Athony Anuman
